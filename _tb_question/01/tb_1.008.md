<!-- skt-start -->

प्रपञ्चेभ्य उपरमणम्। (स्वधर्मानुष्ठानमेव वा।)

<!-- skt-end -->

<!-- eng-start -->

uparati is desisting from prapañca (i.e. from vaidika karma through sannyāsa). Or, it is performing ones dharma (i.e. 
desisting from adharma).

<!-- eng-end -->
