<!-- skt-start -->

 इह स्रक्चन्दनवनितादिषु अमुत्र स्वर्गभोगेष्विच्छाराहित्यम्।

<!-- skt-end -->

<!-- eng-start -->

virāga (dispassion) is being devoice of icchā 
both here (eg. garlands, candana, women etc) and in the here-after (eg. pleasures of svarga).

<!-- eng-end -->
