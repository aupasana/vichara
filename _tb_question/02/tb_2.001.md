<!-- skt-start -->

आत्मा सत्यस्तदन्यत् सर्वं मिथ्येति ज्ञानमेव।

<!-- skt-end -->

<!-- eng-start -->

ātma is eternally and independently true. 
Everything else is mithyā (dependent). 
This jñāna is tattva viveka.

<!-- eng-end -->
