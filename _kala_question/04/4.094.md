देहे अहंतायाः गृहादिषु ममतायाश्च अभिमानकर्ता इन्द्रियद्वारा बहिर्गमनस्य कारणं मनः। 

<!-- split -->

With I-ness of the body and My-ness of the house etc, the manas is the abhimāna kartā and the means through which the indriyas go outwards and function.
