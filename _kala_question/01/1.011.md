“अहं सच्चिदानन्दरूपं ब्रह्म” इति अपरोक्षब्रह्मज्ञानम्। 

<!-- split -->

The aparokṣa (direct) brahma jñāna is of the form “I am brahman of the nature of sat, cit and ānanda.”
