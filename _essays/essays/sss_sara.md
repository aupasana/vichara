## śaṅkara vedānta sāra

The śaṅkara vedānta sāra by sw. saccidānandendra sarasvatī is a 
prakaraṇa grantha. Even though there are copious differences
between the siddhānta and his views (no mūlāvidyā etc), 
he anchors his interpretation to the bhāṣya. This book in particular
is well organized and has many clearly organized extracts from the
bhāṣya. Based on it's sanskrit translation by sw. advayānandendra sarasvatī, a summary of topics is given below.
