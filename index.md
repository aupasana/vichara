## The vicāra candrodaya and vicāra sāgara

This site is based upon pt. pītāmbar-jī's vicāra candrodaya and pt. niścala dās-jī's 
vicāra sāgara. These two prakaraṇa granthas focus on vedāntic inquiry. 
They provide sādhakas with a clear, firm understanding of the vedānta siddhānta.
