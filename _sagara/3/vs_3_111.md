वेदान्तग्रन्थप्रवृत्तिसंपादकं प्रथमतरङ्गे
यदधिकारिविशेषणमुक्तं साधनचतुष्टयरूपं, 
तत्संपत्तिरेव शिष्यस्य लक्षणं भवति।

<div class="translation-inline" markdown="1">
In the first taraṅga, the sādhana catuṣṭaya sapatti 
was described, as part of describing the anubandha 
catuṣṭaya that impells the adhikārī to take up the 
study of vedānta granthas. That same sampatti is 
the lakṣaṇa for the śiṣya.
</div>
